About the Program

Learn by Building
Real Things

Cognifyz Technologies' Web Development Internship is an immersive, hands-on program designed for students and aspiring developers eager to build industry-relevant skills.

Over the course of 8+ weeks, interns progress through three structured levels — from core HTML/CSS/JS fundamentals all the way to advanced front-end frameworks, responsive design, and image manipulation — each building on the last.

You'll work on real deliverables, receive expert feedback, and emerge with a polished portfolio to showcase to future employers. This isn't a lecture series — it's a launchpad.
